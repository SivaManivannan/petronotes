## Use

Chow group is an additional channel in loglog analysis that helps identify transitions in flow regimes.

It is dimensionless.
## Equations

$$ Chow = \frac{\Delta P}{2\frac{d(\Delta P)}{d(ln\Delta t)}} $$

$\Delta P$ in the equation above would be different for PTA and RTA
