## Definitions

Test parameters are a collection of inputs to a PTA or RTA analysis whose values remain constant during the life for the well.

- Well radius $r_w$ is the radius of the well. It is used to compute the surface area through which fluids enter or leave the wellbore
- Pay zone $h$ is the total thickness of the reservoir layers that is in communication with the wellbore (?).
- Porosity $\phi$ is the porosity of the reservoir rock that contains fluids that flow in or out of the wellbore. It is the ratio of the volume of the pores containing fluids to the volume of the rock mass.
- Rock compressibility $c_r$ is the compressibility of the reservoir rock described above

## Intuitions

Among these test parameters pay zone has the strongest influence on PTA results like permeability and skin

Well radius and porosity play a role in the speed of transmission of the pressure waves in the reservoir and play a more important role in the estimation of boundaries and reserves calculation.

The impact of rock compressibility is quite limited as the hydrocarbon compressibility often overpower them in the computation of total compressibility.
