# Critical Pressure

- For a [[Single Component Systems]], this is the pressure above which any gas/liquid mixture cannot coexist regardless of temperature.

# Critical Temperature

- For a [[Single Component Systems]], this is the temperature above which any gas/liquid mixture cannot coexist regardless of pressure.

# Critical Point

At the critical point, the vapor and liquid phases can no longer be distinguished and their intensive properties are identical, both for single and multi-component system.

For [[Single Component Systems]], the vapor-pressure curve terminates at critical point. However, for [[Two Component Systems|Multi Component Systems]], the two region phase can extend beyond the critical point, i.e. at pressures greater than the critical pressure and temperatures greater than the critical temperature.
